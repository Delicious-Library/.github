## What Is Delicious Library for Mac

<a href="#">Delicious Library mac</a> is a media cataloging application for macOS that turns your shelves
of books, movies, music, video games and gadgets into a browsable digital library. Instead of a plain
spreadsheet of titles, it presents your collection as a visual card-catalog — rows of realistic cover
art on wooden shelves — so finding, sorting and admiring what you own feels natural. You can run your
very own library from home or office using a deliberately simple interface.

The core idea behind this <a href="#">media library app mac</a> is speed of entry. A scan of a barcode
is all it needs to add an item, after which it downloads a wealth of information from the internet —
the author, release date, current value, a full description and even a high-resolution picture of the
cover. What used to be a tedious data-entry chore becomes a quick sweep of barcodes past your camera,
and your shelves fill themselves in.

## Import by Barcode, Fast

The signature feature of <a href="#">download delicious library for mac</a> is its full-speed barcode
importer. You do not need dedicated hardware — any QuickTime-supported digital video camera works,
including the built-in Apple iSight.

- **iSight video scanning** — hold a barcode on the back of any book, movie, CD or game in front of the camera and the item appears on your shelf seconds later.
- **CCD-grade decoding** — the same technology found in expensive industrial CCD scanners reads every frame of digital video, seeking, targeting and instantly decoding any visible barcode.
- **Hardware scanner support** — pair a Flic Wireless Laser Bar Code Scanner for rapid, cordless entry.
- **Manual entry** — for items without a barcode, type the title by hand and let the lookup fill in the rest.

Because this <a href="#">catalog collection mac</a> workflow reads continuously, you can import at a
remarkable pace — roughly 750 items an hour if you can move your arms fast enough — so even a large
collection goes from shelf to screen in a single sitting.
